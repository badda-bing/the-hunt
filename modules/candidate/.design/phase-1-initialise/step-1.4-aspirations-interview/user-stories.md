# Step 1.4 — User stories

## US-1.4.1 — Start a 30-min aspirations interview with Oprah

**As a candidate,** after confirming my timeline, I see a call-to-action to start a 30-minute interview with Oprah about my aspirations. Clicking it begins a live, conversational chat session.

**Why it matters:** Vision — *"the direction they're reaching for"*. Aspirations are the forward anchor that points all Phase 2 research.

**Related tech stories:** TS-1.4.1, TS-1.4.2
**Related features:** F-1.4.1, F-1.4.2
**Tests:** see `tests.md` §Positive-US-1.4.1 / §Negative-US-1.4.1

---

## US-1.4.2 — Interview covers vision then next-role specifics

**As a candidate,** Oprah's interview has two clear parts: the first 15 minutes explore my long-term vision (where I'm heading, what I want to build toward); the second 15 minutes pin down concrete next-role specifics (title, seniority, engagement type, must-haves, deal-breakers).

**Why it matters:** Vision — *"deep, exploratory communication"* combined with *"refusing to miss the full picture"*. Both long-term direction and concrete next-role must land.

**Related tech stories:** TS-1.4.1, TS-1.4.3
**Related features:** F-1.4.2, F-1.4.3
**Tests:** see `tests.md` §Positive-US-1.4.2 / §Negative-US-1.4.2

---

## US-1.4.3 — Pause and resume the interview

**As a candidate,** if I need to stop mid-interview, I can pause. When I come back, I can resume from where I left off — Oprah has the full context and doesn't re-ask what I already answered.

**Why it matters:** Vision — *"minimising the candidate's time"* + *"never covering the same ground twice"*.

**Related tech stories:** TS-1.4.2
**Related features:** F-1.4.3
**Tests:** see `tests.md` §Positive-US-1.4.3 / §Negative-US-1.4.3

---

## US-1.4.4 — My aspirations captured with citations to my answers

**As a candidate,** after the interview ends, I see my aspirations and next-role expectations rendered as structured records. Every record cites the specific turn in the transcript where I said it.

**Why it matters:** Vision commitment 1 (Provenance). My answers are what I said; the citations prove it.

**Related tech stories:** TS-1.4.4
**Related features:** F-1.4.4, F-1.4.5
**Tests:** see `tests.md` §Positive-US-1.4.4 / §Negative-US-1.4.4

---

## US-1.4.5 — Know the interview is complete

**As a candidate,** when Oprah has covered both parts and captured enough signal, she tells me we're done. I see a summary of what was captured and can proceed to Step 1.5 (Phase 1 complete).

**Why it matters:** Vision — *"rigorous and measurable"*. The module knows when it has enough and says so.

**Related tech stories:** TS-1.4.3, TS-1.4.5
**Related features:** F-1.4.4
